What is palindrome ?

A palindrome is a word, phrase, number, or other sequence of characters that can be read the same way in either direction, whether forward or backward. This means that the characters are symmetrical and create the same word or phrase regardless of the direction of reading. Palindromes can be found in many languages and can range from simple words like "mom" or "radar" to more complex phrases or sentences such as "A santa at NASA" or like a number "1881". Palindromes are often used in wordplay, riddles, and puzzles.
